• Introduction: -

   The Remote Laboratory represents an extension to the ways in which people use the internet. Most people use the internet to search for information, or perhaps make it available to others. The Remote Laboratory empowers students collect information from the physical world. Remote computer access to laboratory instruments allows undergraduate students to conduct actual experiments at any time and from any location. This technology greatly enhances the flexibility of laboratory education, and introduces students to the new paradigm of remote experimentation. Remote laboratory (also known as online laboratory) is the use of telecommunications to remotely conduct real (as opposed to virtual) experiments, at the physical location of the operating technology, whilst the scientist is utilizing technology from a separate geographical location. Remote laboratory comprehends one or more remote experiments.

• Problem Statement:-

   To design and develop the laboratory in such a way that the students can do the experiment from their desired location at the desired time so that the students can get practical knowledge instead of only theoretical knowledge.

• Literature Survey: -

   Lab-based courses play's an important role in every student's life. It is also said that “hands-on experience is at the heart of science learning. Lab courses have a strong impact on student's learning outcomes as student's actually evaluate the things and see how the things happen.

    The technology has become advanced in each and every field and Researchers have convincingly argued that information technology is changing the laboratory education. Mainly in electronics the nature and practices of laboratories has been changed by two new technology that is intensive automations and simulations. The relative effectiveness of the two new laboratories compared with traditional hands-on labs is rarely explored.

    First, research may look at hybrids of laboratories that are designed accomplish a portfolio of educational objectives. There is a fair amount of evidence that simulated and remote labs are effective in teaching concepts. There is still a shortage of data on whether such technologies are as effective as hands-on laboratories when it comes to teaching design skills. Those who advocate the use of hands-on labs might be more amenable to the use of simulated or remote technologies if all three technologies are clearly integrated as part of a curriculum in which goals such as teaching open-ended ACM Computing Surveys. Right now, the perception is that new technologies are competitors against current teaching techniques, and this sense is probably getting in the way of a more carefully considered educational evaluation. Second, the effectiveness of laboratories may be affected by how much students believe in them. Therefore, an understanding of presence, interaction, and belief may lead to better interfaces. Also, if belief proves important, then hybridapproaches might be contemplated, in which hands-on work is used at an early stage to build confidence in remote or simulated technology used in later teaching. Third, research might pay more attention to collaboration and sensemaking. The technology may change the way we can and should coordinate our work, and studies of such interactions may be productive in suggesting the kinds of educational processes that should accompany the lab technologies.

• Components Description

A) Hardware:

1. ESP8266: -

• The ESP8266 is a low-cost Wi-Fi development board, having tensilica L106 as microcontroller with built-in TCP/IP networking software, and high capability.
• The ESP8266, designed and manufactured by Espressif Systems, contains the crucialelements of a computer: CPU, RAM, networking (WIFI), and even a modern operating system and SDK.
• That makes it an excellent choice for Internet of Things (IoT) projects of all kinds.

2. Relay Module (generic): -

• Relay is an electromechanical device that uses an electric current to open or close the contacts of a switch.
• The single-channel relay module is much more than just a plain relay, it comprises of components that make switching and connection easier and act as indicators to show if the module is powered and if the relay is active or not.
• Relay module have relay trigger pin for the input to active relay.

3. Op-Amp (LM741)

• Op-Amp IC 741 or LM741 is one of the most used operational amplifier integrated circuits that perform both mathematical operations and amplification functions. This small chip mainly performs mathematical operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, differentiation, integration, etc. in various circuits.
• It is a high gain amplifier made up of BJT or FETS, which is often powered by both positive and negative supply voltage. It was first designed by Fairchild Semiconductor in the year 1963.
• In this guide, we will learn about the Operational Amplifier IC or Op-Amp 741 or LM741 basics, characteristics, circuits, pins configuration & Applications. You can also check the LM358 Dual Audio Amplifier IC which has a pair of Op-Amp Circuit.

3.Software:

The website will consist of login page in which the user will get two option weather to signing or signup. If the user is visiting for first time, then he will do signup. On clicking signup, he will be redirected to the page that takes the email id of the user as the input. After entering email-id the user will receive OTP on his entered email-id and he has to enter the OTP correctly then his account will be created.

If the email id and password match to the email id and password stored in database then we will open the menu page else user will get the message enter the details correctly.We will store the Gmail of all the users and the password entered by them using MySQL database where Gmail will be the primary key so that it should be unique i.e., one email-id will be valid for only one user. On reentering the same e-mail id, the user will get message the email id is already registered. Upon successful login user has to set the unique password which will be require whenever he/she wants to login. We will develop the website in such a way that the user will see the list of all experiments. Upon choosing one experiment the calendar will be available in which the user has to select date from the calendar that will be displayed on website. And on the selected date and time he will be able to perform his experiment.
